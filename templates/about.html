About

This is a simple app that uses a publically avaliable dataset on the Queensland Open Data Portal (Specifically the Queensland Fire and Emergency Services - Rural Fire Stations dataset) to find the closest rural fire station given an address. It uses the photon API to find the lat long coordinates of a given address, and then the closest rural firestation from the csv data.
